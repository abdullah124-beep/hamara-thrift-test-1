Our Promise

At Hamara Thrift, we photograph and describe every pair honestly. We are a small home-based business and we take our reputation seriously. If a shoe genuinely does not match what we listed and showed you, we will exchange it for another pair of equal or greater value. We do not offer cash refunds, but we will always make it right with an exchange.

When You Can Exchange

All of the following must be true:

  • You contact us within 3 days of receiving the order.
  • The shoe's condition does not match what was shown in our listing photos or description.
  • The shoe has not been worn by you — same condition as when received.
  • The shoe has not been damaged or altered by you in any way.
  • You have proof of purchase (order confirmation or our WhatsApp conversation).
  • Any original laces, box, or accessories that came with the shoe are included.

When Exchange Is Not Possible

  • More than 3 days have passed since you received the order.
  • The shoe has been worn, used, or washed by you.
  • The shoe has been damaged or altered after you received it.
  • You simply changed your mind — we sell unique one-of-a-kind pairs, so please review listings carefully before ordering.
  • The condition was clearly described and visible in our listing photos.
  • We have no available pair to exchange with at the time of your request.

How to Request an Exchange

  1. Message us on WhatsApp within 3 days of delivery. Send your order details and clear photos showing the issue.
  2. We will review and reply within 24 hours.
  3. If approved, we will agree on a replacement pair from our current stock. If no matching pair is available, we will hold a store credit for you until one is.
  4. You send the original pair back via TCS. Shipping costs for approved exchanges are covered by us if the fault is on our side.
  5. Once we receive and inspect the pair, we dispatch the replacement.

Important Notes

  • No cash refunds — we only offer exchanges or store credit.
  • Exchange is for a pair of equal or greater value. If the replacement costs more, you pay the difference.
  • We are a home-based thrift store. Every pair is unique and listed honestly. We strongly encourage you to ask us any questions on WhatsApp before ordering if you are unsure about a pair.
  • By purchasing from Hamara Thrift you agree to this exchange policy.

Have a question before ordering?

Message us on WhatsApp anytime — we usually reply within 30 minutes and are happy to answer any questions about a pair before you buy.
