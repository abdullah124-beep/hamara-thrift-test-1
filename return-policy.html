Our Return Guarantee

At Hamara Thrift, we photograph and describe every pair with complete honesty. If something isn't right — if the shoe doesn't match what we showed you — we will make it right. No questions asked.

When You Can Return

You are eligible for a return if all of the following apply:

  • You contact us within 3 days of receiving the order.
  • The shoe's condition does not match what was listed and shown in photos.
  • The shoe has not been worn by you — it must be in the same condition as received.
  • The shoe has not been damaged by you — no new scuffs, stains, or alterations.
  • You have proof of purchase (order confirmation or WhatsApp conversation).
  • The shoe is returned with any original packaging or extras that came with it.

When Returns Are Not Accepted

  • More than 3 days have passed since delivery.
  • The shoe has been worn or used by you after receiving it.
  • The shoe has been damaged, altered, or cleaned by you in a way that changes its condition.
  • You simply changed your mind (we sell one-of-a-kind pairs — please review listings carefully before ordering).
  • The issue was clearly described and shown in our listing photos and description.
  • The shoe was purchased during a final sale or marked as non-returnable at time of sale.

How to Return

  1. Message us on WhatsApp within 3 days of delivery with your order details and photos of the issue.
  2. We'll review and respond within 24 hours with instructions.
  3. If approved, you'll send the shoe back via TCS. Return shipping costs are covered by Hamara Thrift if the issue is our fault.
  4. Once we receive and inspect the returned pair, we'll issue a full refund or arrange an exchange — your choice.

Refunds

Refunds are issued via the same payment method used for the order (EasyPaisa, JazzCash, or bank transfer). For Cash on Delivery orders, we'll transfer the amount directly to your EasyPaisa or JazzCash account. Refunds are processed within 2–3 business days of us receiving the returned item.
