Our Story

From a passion for
sneakers to a movement

It started with a cupboard full of sneakers and a simple question: why do perfectly good shoes end up in the trash? In Multan, we built Hamara Thrift to answer that question — connecting sneaker lovers with premium pairs at prices that actually make sense.

Every pair we list is hand-picked, cleaned, and graded honestly. We believe second-hand doesn't mean second-best — and happy customers across Pakistan agree.
