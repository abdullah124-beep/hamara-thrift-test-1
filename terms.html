Last updated: January 1, 2026

By purchasing from Hamara Thrift Multan (hamarathrift.pk), you agree to these Terms and Conditions. Please read them carefully before placing an order.

1. About Us

Hamara Thrift Multan is a second-hand / pre-loved sneaker business based in Multan, Punjab, Pakistan. We are a locally operated small business specializing in the curated resale of premium athletic footwear. Our contact address is Wapda Town, Multan, Punjab 60000.

2. Product Descriptions and Condition Grading

We take great care to accurately describe each item listed on our website. All products are graded using our standardized condition system:

  • New: Unworn or worn maximum 1-2 times, with no visible wear. May include original box.
  • Excellent: Lightly used, minimal signs of wear, near-perfect condition.
  • Good: Normal use visible but structurally sound and clean.
  • Fair: More significant wear visible; functions perfectly but shows age.

We make every effort to photograph real flaws. However, minor discrepancies in color may occur due to screen calibration differences. If you receive a product that does not match its listed condition, you are entitled to a return under our Returns Policy.

3. Pricing

All prices are listed in Pakistani Rupees (PKR) and are inclusive of any applicable taxes. Prices are subject to change at any time without prior notice. The price you see at the time of placing your order is the price you will pay. Delivery charges are additional and calculated at checkout.

4. Orders and Payment

By placing an order on our website, you are making a binding offer to purchase the selected product(s). We reserve the right to accept or decline orders at our sole discretion. Orders are confirmed via WhatsApp after placement. We currently accept the following payment methods:

  • Cash on Delivery (COD) — for Multan city orders only
  • EasyPaisa / JazzCash
  • Bank transfer (details provided on confirmation)

5. Delivery

We deliver within Multan city on the same day for orders placed before 2:00 PM. Orders placed after 2:00 PM are delivered the following day. For deliveries outside Multan, we use third-party courier services (TCS, Leopards, M&P) with an estimated delivery time of 2–4 working days. Delivery charges are non-refundable unless the error is on our part.

6. Returns and Exchanges

We offer a 7-day return policy from the date of delivery. To be eligible for a return, the item must be returned in the same condition as received, unworn beyond trying them on. Returns are accepted if:

  • The product condition does not match the listing description
  • You received the wrong item
  • The product has a defect not shown or mentioned in the listing

We do not accept returns for buyer's remorse (change of mind) or size issues if the correct size was clearly listed. To initiate a return, contact us on WhatsApp at +92 300 1234567 within 7 days of delivery with photos of the issue.

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your personal information.

1. Who We Are

Hamara Thrift Multan ("we", "us", "our") is a second-hand sneaker business based in Multan, Pakistan. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit our website or place an order with us.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Order information: Name, phone number, delivery address, order details
  • Contact information: Email address, phone number (when you contact us)
  • Usage data: Pages visited, time on site, browser type (collected anonymously via analytics)
  • Cart data: Products added to cart (stored locally in your browser via localStorage — not on our servers)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information solely to:

  • Process and fulfill your orders
